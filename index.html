Sig Fig Rules (Fast Reference)

  • Addition/Subtraction: round to the least number of decimal places among inputs.
  • Multiplication/Division: round to the least number of significant figures among inputs.
  • Leading zeros: not significant. Trailing zeros after a decimal are significant.

Learn more: sig fig rules and examples.

Examples

Addition: 12.3 + 1.45 = 13.75 → round to one decimal → 13.8.

Multiplication: 2.15 × 3.4 = 7.31 → least SF is 2 → 7.3.
